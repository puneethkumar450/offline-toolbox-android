Policy details

Tap each section to expand it.

01 Information we store Local settings and app preferences only +

AI ToolNest stores limited information locally on your device to support app functionality and personalization.

  • Theme and appearance settings
  • Layout preferences and favorites
  • Recent tool usage and timer-related app state
02 Information we do not collect No account-based tracking or profile building +

AI ToolNest does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information for analytics, advertising, or profile building.

03 Permissions Used only when the related feature needs them +

AI ToolNest may request access to the following capabilities when needed:

  • Notifications for timer alerts and countdown updates.
  • Foreground service to keep an active countdown timer running in the background with a visible notification.
  • Camera flash capability for the flashlight tool on supported devices.
04 How information is used To power features and remember preferences +

Any locally stored data is used only to provide app features and remember your preferences on your device.

05 Data sharing and security No sale of personal data, with an offline-first design +

AI ToolNest does not sell your personal information and does not share personal data with third parties.

The app is designed around offline functionality, so most app data remains on your device.

06 Children's privacy and policy updates Designed without intentional child data collection +

AI ToolNest is not designed to intentionally collect personal information from children.

This privacy policy may be updated if app features or data practices change.
