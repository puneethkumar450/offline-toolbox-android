AI ToolNest Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

ToolNest is an offline utilities app that provides productivity, finance, developer, and device tools directly on your Android device.

Information We Store

ToolNest stores limited app information locally on your device to support app functionality and personalization. This may include settings such as theme, layout preferences, favorite tools, recent tools, and timer-related app state.

Information We Do Not Collect

ToolNest does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information for analytics, advertising, or profile building.

Permissions

ToolNest may request access to the following device capabilities when needed:

  • Notifications for timer alerts and countdown updates.
  • Foreground service to keep an active countdown timer running in the background with a visible notification.
  • Camera flash capability for the flashlight tool on supported devices.

How Information Is Used

Any locally stored data is used only to provide app features and remember your preferences on your device.

Data Sharing

ToolNest does not sell your personal information and does not share personal data with third parties.

Data Security

ToolNest is designed around offline functionality, so most app data remains on your device.

Children's Privacy

ToolNest is not designed to intentionally collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if app features or data practices change.
